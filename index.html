chef-mailhog

This cookbook installs MailHog.

Usage

Include the mailhog recipe to install MailHog on your system:

include_recipe "mailhog"

MailHog service is installed and managed via runit.

Requirements

Cookbooks:

  • runit

Platforms:

  • Ubuntu
  • Debian
  • RHEL
  • CentOS
  • Fedora
